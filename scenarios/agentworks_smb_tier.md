# Hypothetical release under test: "AgentWorks Go" — agentic SOC for the sub-500-seat market

CrowdStrike is considering an SMB tier of Charlotte AI AgentWorks, bundled into
Falcon Go/Pro and MSP offerings:

- Three pre-built, locked-configuration agents: phishing-report triage, alert
  triage with plain-English verdict summaries, and a weekly "state of your
  security" email written for owners/IT generalists.
- No-code customization limited to notification routing and approval thresholds;
  every automated action is default read-only until an explicit human approval,
  with a full plain-language audit trail.
- Multi-tenant controls for MSP partners: one console, per-client guardrails,
  margin-friendly per-seat pricing.
- Pricing: included at no extra cost up to 100 endpoints; flat $99/month per
  tenant beyond that. No per-action or per-token metering.

Question for the panel: would you (or your MSP) turn this on, and what would
stop you?
